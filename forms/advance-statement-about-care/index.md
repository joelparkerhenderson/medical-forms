# Advance Statement About Care

Document for recording a patient's wishes and preferences about future care, including values, beliefs, care preferences, medical treatment wishes, communication preferences, people important to them, and practical matters, with signature and witness capture. Assessed for completeness.

## Scoring system

- **Instrument**: Completeness Score
- **Range**: Three-level categorical
- **Categories**: Complete / Partial / Incomplete

## Steps

| #   | Step                         |
| --- | ---------------------------- |
| 1   | Personal Information         |
| 2   | Statement Context            |
| 3   | Values & Beliefs             |
| 4   | Care Preferences             |
| 5   | Medical Treatment Wishes     |
| 6   | Communication Preferences    |
| 7   | People Important to Me       |
| 8   | Practical Matters            |
| 9   | Signatures & Witnesses       |
