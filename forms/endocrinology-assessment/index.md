# Endocrinology Assessment

General endocrinology consultation assessment covering the thyroid, adrenal, glucose, reproductive, pituitary, and bone/calcium axes, integrating symptom review, clinical examination, and laboratory findings to characterise endocrine disturbance and guide management.

## Scoring system

- **Instrument**: Endocrine Symptom & Biochemical Review (aligned to NICE endocrinology guidelines)
- **Range**: Normal / Subclinical / Mild / Moderate / Severe disturbance per axis
- **Categories** (per axis):
  - Normal: Clinical and biochemical indices within reference range
  - Subclinical: Biochemical abnormality without symptoms
  - Mild / Moderate / Severe: Progressive symptomatic disturbance requiring treatment escalation

## Steps

| #   | Step                                                          |
| --- | ------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 1   | Demographics                                                  |
| 2   | Presenting Symptoms                                           |
| 3   | Thyroid Axis Review (TSH, FT4, FT3, antibodies)               |
| 4   | Adrenal Axis Review (cortisol, ACTH, aldosterone)             |
| 5   | Glucose Metabolism (HbA1c, fasting glucose)                   |
| 6   | Reproductive Axis (FSH, LH, testosterone/oestradiol)          |
| 7   | Pituitary Function (prolactin, IGF-1, GH)                     |
| 8   | Bone & Calcium (PTH, vitamin D, calcium)                      |
| 9   | Medications & Lifestyle Review                                |
| 10  | Clinical Impression & Management Plan                         |
