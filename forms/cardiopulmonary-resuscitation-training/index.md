# Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation Training

Basic Life Support (BLS) cardiopulmonary resuscitation competency assessment aligned with AHA/Resuscitation Council (UK) guidelines, used to certify rescuers on adult BLS and AED skills with pass/fail criteria and critical-action review.

## Scoring system

- **Instrument**: AHA BLS Skills Verification Checklist
- **Range**: Pass / Fail (with critical-action criteria)
- **Categories**:
  - Pass: All required skills demonstrated and no critical-action failure
  - Fail: Any critical-action failure OR more than two non-critical deficiencies; remediation required

## Steps

| #   | Step                                         |
| --- | -------------------------------------------- |
| 1   | Trainee Details                              |
| 2   | Scene Safety & Initial Assessment            |
| 3   | Responsiveness & Breathing Check             |
| 4   | Activate Emergency Response                  |
| 5   | Chest Compressions (rate, depth, recoil)     |
| 6   | Airway & Rescue Breaths                      |
| 7   | AED Use & Shock Delivery                     |
| 8   | Team Dynamics, Handoff & Post-Event Feedback |
