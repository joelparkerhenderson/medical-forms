# Sports Medicine Assessment

Pre-participation physical evaluation (PPE) for athletes and sportspeople, aligned with the AAP/AAFP/ACSM/AMSSM/AOSSM/AOASM Pre-Participation Physical Evaluation (5th ed.) monograph, to clear participation, identify risk, and plan follow-up.

## Scoring system

- **Instrument**: Pre-Participation Physical Evaluation (5th ed.)
- **Range**: Cleared / Cleared with Conditions / Not Cleared Pending Further Evaluation / Not Cleared for Sport
- **Categories**:
  - Cleared: No concerns; full participation
  - Cleared with Conditions: Participation permitted with named restrictions or accommodations
  - Not Cleared Pending Further Evaluation: Temporary hold pending investigation (e.g. abnormal cardiac finding)
  - Not Cleared for Sport: Significant condition contraindicating participation in chosen sport

## Steps

| #   | Step                                                                    |
| --- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 1   | Demographics                                                            |
| 2   | Sport & Position Details                                                |
| 3   | Medical History (cardiovascular, concussion, asthma, musculoskeletal)   |
| 4   | Family History (SCD, inherited cardiac conditions)                      |
| 5   | Menstrual History / RED-S Screening (if applicable)                     |
| 6   | Cardiovascular Screening (14-point AHA)                                 |
| 7   | Musculoskeletal Screening                                               |
| 8   | Neurological & Concussion Baseline (SCAT6)                              |
| 9   | Vision & Skin                                                           |
| 10  | Clearance Decision                                                      |
