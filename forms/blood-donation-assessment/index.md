# Blood Donation Assessment

Blood donation eligibility screening aligned with the JPAC Donor Selection Guidelines used by UK NHSBT, covering donor demographics, health status, medications, recent illness, travel history, lifestyle risk, and vital signs to determine donation eligibility.

## Scoring system

- **Instrument**: JPAC Donor Selection Guidelines (DSG)
- **Range**: Eligible / Temporarily Deferred / Permanently Deferred
- **Categories**:
  - Eligible: No deferral criteria triggered; donor may proceed
  - Temporarily Deferred: Time-limited deferral (e.g., recent travel, recent illness, pregnancy)
  - Permanently Deferred: Lifelong deferral (e.g., certain infections, CJD risk, some medication histories)

## Steps

| #   | Step                                               |
| --- | -------------------------------------------------- |
| 1   | Donor Demographics                                 |
| 2   | General Health & Wellbeing                         |
| 3   | Medical History (chronic conditions, medications)  |
| 4   | Recent Illness & Infections                        |
| 5   | Travel History (malaria, WNV, vCJD risk areas)     |
| 6   | Lifestyle & Risk Behaviours                        |
| 7   | Pregnancy & Transfusion History                    |
| 8   | Vital Signs (Hb, blood pressure, pulse)            |
| 9   | Informed Consent                                   |
| 10  | Donation Plan                                      |
