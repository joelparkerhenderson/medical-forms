# Emergency Medical Technician Psychomotor Examination

NREMT-style psychomotor skills examination for Emergency Medical Technicians, scored against a point-based checklist for scene size-up, primary survey, history taking, secondary assessment, and reassessment, with explicit critical-criteria failure conditions.

## Scoring system

- **Instrument**: NREMT Psychomotor Skills Examination (patient assessment station)
- **Range**: Pass / Fail with critical-criteria overrides
- **Categories**:
  - Pass: Minimum point threshold achieved and no critical-criteria failure
  - Fail: Any critical-criteria failure OR total points below minimum threshold
- **Critical criteria** (any triggers automatic Fail): failure to take PPE precautions; failure to determine scene safety; failure to voice and provide oxygen; failure to manage airway/breathing/shock; failure to differentiate transport urgency; ordering a dangerous intervention; failure to provide spinal protection when indicated; failure to initiate or call for transport within 15 minutes
