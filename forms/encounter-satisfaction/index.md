# Encounter Satisfaction

Patient encounter satisfaction survey for collecting feedback on healthcare experiences.

## Scoring system

- **Instrument**: Encounter Satisfaction Score (ESS), inspired by PSQ-18 and HCAHPS
- **Scale**: 5-point Likert (1=Very Dissatisfied ... 5=Very Satisfied)
- **Range**: 1.0 - 5.0 (composite mean)
- **Categories**:
  - 4.5 - 5.0: Excellent
  - 3.5 - 4.4: Good
  - 2.5 - 3.4: Fair
  - 1.5 - 2.4: Poor
  - 1.0 - 1.4: Very Poor

## Steps (8 total)

1. Demographics
2. Visit Information
3. Access & Scheduling (3 Likert questions)
4. Communication (4 Likert questions)
5. Staff & Professionalism (3 Likert questions)
6. Care Quality (3 Likert questions)
7. Environment (3 Likert questions)
8. Overall Satisfaction (3 Likert questions + free-text comments)
