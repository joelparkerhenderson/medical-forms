# WHO Acute Referral Form

World Health Organization standardised acute referral form for inter-facility patient transfers.

## Source

- **Form**: Acute Referral Form
- **Organisation**: World Health Organization (WHO)

## Form sections (SBAR framework)

- **Patient Identification**: Name, DOB, sex, contacts
- **Facility & Transport**: Initiating facility, referral facility, ambulance, mode of transfer, times
- **Situation**: Chief complaint, primary diagnosis, pregnancy, other diagnoses, treatments initiated
- **Background**: History of present illness, past medical/surgical history, ABCDE assessment with interventions
- **Assessment**: Clinical assessment with current vital signs, need for referral
- **Recommendations**: Treatment plan during transport, potential worsening, cautions, precautions
- **Provider Sign-off**: Initiating facility provider name + signature
- **Referral Facility Receipt**: Arrival time, receiving provider, feedback checkbox

## Assessment steps (8 total)

1. Patient Identification - name, DOB, sex, contacts
2. Facility & Transport - facilities, ambulance, mode, times
3. Situation - complaint, diagnosis, pregnancy, treatments
4. Background - history, ABCDE assessment
5. Assessment - clinical assessment with vital signs
6. Recommendations - transport plan, precautions
7. Provider Sign-off - initiating facility signature
8. Referral Facility Receipt - arrival, receiving provider

## Two-party completion

- **Initiating facility** completes: patient ID, facilities, situation, background, assessment, recommendations, provider sign-off
- **Referral facility** completes: arrival time, receiving provider, feedback checkbox

## Precaution flags

- Highly infectious disease
- Spinal precautions
- Weight bearing restrictions
- Fall risk
- Aspiration risk
- Other

## Key domain terms

- **SBAR**: Situation, Background, Assessment, Recommendations — communication framework
- **ABCDE**: Airway, Breathing, Circulation, Disability, Exposure
- **Initiating facility**: Facility referring the patient
- **Referral facility**: Facility receiving and providing referral care
