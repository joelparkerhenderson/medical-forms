# Obstetrics Assessment

Antenatal obstetric assessment aligned with NICE NG201, stratifying pregnancies into low, moderate, and high risk to allocate care pathway (midwifery-led, obstetrician-led, or multidisciplinary) and to schedule surveillance, screening, and birth planning.

## Scoring system

- **Instrument**: NICE NG201 Antenatal Risk Assessment
- **Range**: Low Risk / Moderate Risk / High Risk
- **Categories**:
  - Low Risk: No obstetric, medical, or social risk factors — midwifery-led care
  - Moderate Risk: Modifiable risk factors requiring obstetric input at key milestones
  - High Risk: Significant pre-existing or pregnancy-related pathology — consultant-led / multidisciplinary care

## Steps

| #   | Step                                                      |
| --- | --------------------------------------------------------- |
| 1   | Maternal Demographics                                     |
| 2   | Obstetric History                                         |
| 3   | Medical History (cardiac, hypertension, diabetes, VTE)    |
| 4   | Current Pregnancy Details (LMP, EDD, dating scan)         |
| 5   | Lifestyle & Social Factors                                |
| 6   | Screening Test Results (combined test, GTT, anomaly scan) |
| 7   | Mental Health Assessment (Whooley, GAD-2)                 |
| 8   | Fetal Assessment (growth, position, movements)            |
| 9   | Birth Preferences                                         |
| 10  | Care Plan & Follow-up                                     |
