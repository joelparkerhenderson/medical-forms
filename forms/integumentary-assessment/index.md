# Integumentary Assessment

Structured integumentary (skin, hair, nails) clinical assessment combining a head-to-toe skin inspection with the Braden Scale for pressure ulcer risk and wound TIME assessment, to grade pressure-ulcer risk and characterise integumentary findings.

## Scoring system

- **Instrument**: Braden Scale for Pressure Ulcer Risk + Integumentary System Review
- **Range**: Braden 6-23 (lower = higher risk); wound stage (if present)
- **Categories (Braden Scale)**:
  - Very High Risk: ≤ 9
  - High Risk: 10-12
  - Moderate Risk: 13-14
  - Mild Risk: 15-18
  - No Risk: 19-23

## Steps

| #   | Step                                                                        |
| --- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 1   | Demographics                                                                |
| 2   | Presenting Skin Concern                                                     |
| 3   | Skin Inspection (colour, moisture, integrity, turgor, lesions)              |
| 4   | Hair & Scalp Examination                                                    |
| 5   | Nail Examination                                                            |
| 6   | Wound Assessment (if applicable — stage, TIME)                              |
| 7   | Braden Scale Scoring (sensory, moisture, activity, mobility, nutrition, friction/shear) |
| 8   | Photography & Documentation                                                 |
| 9   | Clinical Impression & Care Plan                                             |
