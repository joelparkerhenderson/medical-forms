# Attention Deficit Assessment

ADHD screening using the ASRS (Adult ADHD Self-Report Scale) v1.1 screener.

## Scoring system

- **Instrument**: ASRS Screener (Adult ADHD Self-Report Scale v1.1)
- **Range**: Part A (6 questions, screener) + Part B (12 questions, supplemental)
- **Categories**: Part A items scored against clinically validated thresholds; 4+ darkly shaded responses in Part A = highly consistent with ADHD diagnosis

## Steps

| #   | Step                 | Section                                                  |
| --- | -------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------- |
| 1   | Demographics         | Patient demographics and identification                  |
| 2   | ASRS Part A Screener | Six-question ADHD screening instrument                   |
| 3   | ASRS Part B          | Twelve supplemental symptom frequency questions          |
| 4   | Childhood History    | Symptoms and behaviours in childhood                     |
| 5   | Functional Impact    | Impact on work, relationships, and daily life            |
| 6   | Comorbid Conditions  | Screening for co-occurring conditions                    |
| 7   | Current Medications  | Current medications and supplements                      |
| 8   | Allergies            | Known allergies and adverse reactions                    |
| 9   | Medical History      | Past medical history and relevant diagnoses              |
| 10  | Social & Support     | Social situation, support network, and coping strategies |
