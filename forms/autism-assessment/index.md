# Autism Assessment

Autism spectrum screening using the AQ-10 questionnaire with sensory and developmental profiling.

## Scoring system

- **Instrument**: AQ-10 Score (Autism Spectrum Quotient - 10 item)
- **Range**: 0-10
- **Categories**: 0-5 = Below threshold, >=6 = Referral for diagnostic assessment recommended

## Steps

| #   | Step                  | Section                                            |
| --- | --------------------- | -------------------------------------------------- |
| 1   | Demographics          | Patient demographics and identification            |
| 2   | Screening Purpose     | Reason for referral and screening context          |
| 3   | AQ-10 Questionnaire   | Ten standardised screening questions               |
| 4   | Social Communication  | Social interaction and communication patterns      |
| 5   | Repetitive Behaviors  | Restricted interests and repetitive behaviours     |
| 6   | Sensory Profile       | Sensory sensitivities and processing differences   |
| 7   | Developmental History | Early development milestones and childhood history |
| 8   | Current Support       | Current support needs and accommodations           |
| 9   | Family History        | Family history of autism and related conditions    |
