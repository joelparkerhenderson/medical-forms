# Learning Disability Assessment

Structured annual health check and adaptive-functioning assessment for people with a learning disability, aligned with the NHS England Annual Health Check and DSM-5-TR / ICD-11 criteria, producing a severity category and a personalised Health Action Plan with reasonable adjustments.

## Scoring system

- **Instrument**: NHS England Annual Health Check + Adaptive Behaviour Scale
- **Range**: Mild / Moderate / Severe / Profound
- **Categories (DSM-5-TR aligned)**:
  - Mild (IQ ~50-69 with limited adaptive deficits): Independent with support in complex tasks
  - Moderate (IQ ~35-49): Needs significant support with daily living
  - Severe (IQ ~20-34): Needs substantial support; limited communication
  - Profound (IQ < 20): Very limited understanding and communication; intensive support

## Steps

| #   | Step                                                         |
| --- | ------------------------------------------------------------ |
| 1   | Demographics                                                 |
| 2   | Carer & Support Network                                      |
| 3   | Communication Needs (easy-read, Makaton, AAC)                |
| 4   | Medical Review (epilepsy, mental health, medications)        |
| 5   | Physical Examination & Observations                          |
| 6   | Adaptive Functioning (conceptual, social, practical domains) |
| 7   | Behavioural Concerns & Triggers                              |
| 8   | Mental Capacity & Consent                                    |
| 9   | Reasonable Adjustments Required                              |
| 10  | Health Action Plan                                           |
