# Palliative Assessment

Symptom-focused palliative care assessment using the Edmonton Symptom Assessment System-revised (ESAS-r) alongside performance status, goals-of-care documentation, medication and symptom-control planning, and psychosocial and spiritual review to guide individualised palliative management.

## Scoring system

- **Instrument**: Edmonton Symptom Assessment System-revised (ESAS-r)
- **Range**: 10 symptoms rated 0-10; total 0-100
- **Categories**:
  - None (0-10): Minimal symptom burden
  - Mild (11-30): Low symptom burden; routine review
  - Moderate (31-60): Meaningful symptom burden; escalation of symptom control
  - Severe (61-100): High symptom burden; urgent review / hospice referral
- **Individual flag**: Any symptom ≥ 7 → urgent single-symptom review regardless of total
