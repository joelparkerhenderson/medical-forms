# Framingham Risk Score for Hard Coronary Heart Disease

Estimates 10-year risk of heart attack (myocardial infarction or coronary death) in patients aged 30-79 years with no history of CHD or diabetes, using the Wilson/D'Agostino 1998 Cox regression model.

## Scoring system

- **Instrument**: Framingham Hard CHD Risk Score
- **Range**: 0-100% (10-year probability)
- **Categories**: <10% = Low, 10-19.9% = Intermediate, >=20% = High

## Steps

| #   | Step                |
| --- | ------------------- |
| 1   | Patient Information |
| 2   | Demographics        |
| 3   | Smoking History     |
| 4   | Blood Pressure      |
| 5   | Cholesterol         |
| 6   | Medical History     |
| 7   | Family History      |
| 8   | Lifestyle Factors   |
| 9   | Current Medications |
| 10  | Review & Calculate  |
