# Audiology Assessment

Comprehensive hearing evaluation covering audiometric testing, tinnitus, vestibular function, and communication impact.

## Scoring system

- **Instrument**: Hearing Level Grade
- **Range**: Multi-level categorical based on pure tone average
- **Categories**: Normal (<=25 dB), Mild (26-40 dB), Moderate (41-55 dB), Moderately Severe (56-70 dB), Severe (71-90 dB), Profound (>90 dB)

## Steps

| #   | Step                       | Section                                                            |
| --- | -------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| 1   | Demographics               | Patient demographics and identification                            |
| 2   | Chief Complaint            | Primary hearing concerns and onset                                 |
| 3   | Hearing History            | Duration, progression, and prior hearing assessments               |
| 4   | Audiometric Results        | Pure tone audiometry, speech recognition, and tympanometry         |
| 5   | Tinnitus Assessment        | Tinnitus presence, characteristics, and severity                   |
| 6   | Vestibular Symptoms        | Dizziness, vertigo, and balance concerns                           |
| 7   | Otoscopic Findings         | External ear and tympanic membrane examination                     |
| 8   | Medical History            | Relevant medical history, noise exposure, and ototoxic medications |
| 9   | Functional & Communication | Impact on communication, social participation, and quality of life |
