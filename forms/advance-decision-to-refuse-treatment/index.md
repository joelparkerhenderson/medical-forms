# Advance Decision to Refuse Treatment

Legal document allowing patients to record treatment refusal decisions in advance, assessed for legal validity per Mental Capacity Act 2005.

## Scoring system

- **Instrument**: Validity Check
- **Range**: Three-level categorical
- **Categories**: Valid, Invalid, Incomplete

## Steps

| #   | Step                                 | Section                                                    |
| --- | ------------------------------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------- |
| 1   | Personal Information                 | Patient demographics and identification                    |
| 2   | Capacity Declaration                 | Confirmation of mental capacity at time of writing         |
| 3   | Circumstances                        | Description of circumstances in which the decision applies |
| 4   | Treatments Refused - General         | Specification of general treatments being refused          |
| 5   | Treatments Refused - Life-Sustaining | Specification of life-sustaining treatments being refused  |
| 6   | Exceptions & Conditions              | Any exceptions or conditions to the refusal                |
| 7   | Other Wishes                         | Additional wishes not covered by specific refusals         |
| 8   | Lasting Power of Attorney            | Details of any existing LPA arrangements                   |
| 9   | Healthcare Professional Review       | Review and verification by a healthcare professional       |
| 10  | Legal Signatures                     | Signatures, witnesses, and legal attestation               |
