# Allergy Assessment

Comprehensive allergy evaluation covering drug, food, and environmental allergies with anaphylaxis risk assessment.

## Scoring system

- **Instrument**: Allergy Severity
- **Range**: Four-level categorical
- **Categories**: Low, Moderate, High, Critical

## Steps

| #   | Step                    | Section                                                             |
| --- | ----------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 1   | Demographics            | Patient demographics and identification                             |
| 2   | Allergy History         | Overview of allergy onset, duration, and patterns                   |
| 3   | Drug Allergies          | Documented drug and medication allergies with reactions             |
| 4   | Food Allergies          | Documented food allergies and intolerances                          |
| 5   | Environmental Allergies | Pollen, dust, mould, pet dander, and other environmental triggers   |
| 6   | Anaphylaxis History     | Previous anaphylactic episodes and emergency responses              |
| 7   | Testing Results         | Skin prick tests, blood tests (specific IgE), and other diagnostics |
| 8   | Current Management      | Current medications, immunotherapy, and avoidance strategies        |
| 9   | Comorbidities           | Related conditions such as asthma, eczema, and rhinitis             |
| 10  | Impact & Action Plan    | Quality of life impact and personalised action plan                 |
